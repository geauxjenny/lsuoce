A Century of Access. A Future of Opportunity.

For more than 100 years, LSU Continuing Education has expanded access to learning across Louisiana and beyond.

What began in 1924 as University Extension has grown into a modern portfolio of professional development, online learning, lifelong learning, and workforce training.

Today, LSU Continuing Education serves learners at every stage through flexible programs offered online, on campus, and on site.

Three Ways LSU Delivers Learning

LSU Continuing Education meets learners and organizations where they are through three flexible delivery models: online, on campus, and on site.

Online

Online learning expands access and flexibility for working adults. LSU offers self-paced online college credit, live-online and online certificate options, and programs designed so learners can start anytime, study anywhere, and build skills without putting life on hold.

On Campus

On-campus learning creates direct, instructor-led experiences at LSU for professionals who want structured, face-to-face development in areas like business, management, communication, project management, and more.

On Site

On-site learning brings LSU directly to employers through custom workforce training. LSU delivers expert-led training at the workplace and can tailor programs in leadership, project management, sales, communication, safety, and related areas to organizational goals.

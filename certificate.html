Learning Pathways

After earning the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate, you will open doors to learning pathways that can enhance your skills and credentials. Through LSU’s Credit for Prior Learning initiative, you can potentially use your certificate to waive certain college credits, eliminating the need to retake courses that demonstrate competencies gained through our certificate programs.

The AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate may contribute toward the following online degrees offered through LSU Online:

  • Bachelor of General Studies in Organizational Studies with LSUA
  • BS in Business Administration with LSUA
  • BS in Health Professions: Health Care Administration with LSUA
  • Master of Business Administration with LSU

Additionally, this certificate is a prerequisite for the AWS Solutions Architect Certificate and leads to the AWS Cloud Computing Specialist Certificate.

Note: Credit for Prior Learning is determined on a case-by-case basis and subject to change. It is evaluated once students are admitted into a degree program.

Frequently Asked Questions

What career opportunities are available after obtaining the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate?

Cloud computing skills are in high demand across industries as organizations continue adopting cloud-based technologies and infrastructure. LSU’s AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate, delivered through LSU Online, can help learners prepare for entry-level and foundational cloud computing roles related to cloud operations, technical support, systems administration, and cloud-based business environments.

Professionals with AWS cloud knowledge may pursue career paths such as Cloud Support Associate, Solutions Engineer, Systems Administrator, Technical Consultant, or Cloud Operations Specialist. The certificate can also serve as a foundation for more advanced AWS certifications and cloud computing career pathways.

What are the costs associated with the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate at LSU?

The cost of LSU’s online AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate may vary depending on whether learners purchase the certificate bundle or enroll in courses individually. Additional costs could include optional certification exam fees, technology requirements, or related learning materials.

Learners should review the LSU Online registration page for the most current pricing information, payment options, and available funding opportunities. LSU Online also provides payment flexibility through employer reimbursement, continuing education financing options, and standard credit card payment methods.

Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate program?

LSU’s online AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate is designed for beginners and does not require prior cloud computing experience or advanced technical knowledge. The program introduces foundational cloud concepts, AWS services, cloud architectures, and core cloud computing principles in an accessible learning format.

Learners should have basic computer skills, internet access, and familiarity with online learning platforms. An interest in cloud technology, IT systems, or digital infrastructure can also help learners succeed in the program.

How does the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate compare to other cloud certification programs?

The AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate focuses specifically on foundational cloud computing concepts and Amazon Web Services (AWS), one of the world’s leading cloud platforms. LSU’s AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate delivered through LSU Online is designed to help learners build practical cloud knowledge while preparing for industry-recognized AWS certification pathways.

Compared to broader or vendor-neutral cloud programs, AWS-focused training provides direct exposure to AWS terminology, services, cloud architecture concepts, and cloud business applications commonly used by organizations worldwide. The program is especially valuable for learners seeking an introduction to cloud computing or planning to pursue advanced AWS certifications in the future.

What specific skills will I learn in the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate program?

LSU’s online AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate helps learners build foundational skills related to cloud computing concepts, AWS services, cloud security, cloud architecture, and cloud-based business solutions. Learners explore cloud characteristics, deployment models, cloud infrastructure, and common AWS applications used in modern organizations.

The program also introduces concepts related to scalability, data storage, cloud networking, operational efficiency, and cloud service management. These skills help learners develop a foundational understanding of how cloud technologies support business innovation and digital transformation.

Are there any live sessions, or is the program entirely self-paced?

LSU’s AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate, delivered through LSU Online, is primarily offered in a self-paced online format through Moodle, LSU’s learning management system. Learners can access course materials, complete modules, and work through assignments on their own schedule within the course completion deadlines.

Although the courses are flexible, all assignments and activities must be completed before the course expiration date. Learners are encouraged to progress steadily through modules each week to stay on track throughout the program.

What support is available for students during the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate program?

Learners enrolled in LSU’s online AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate have access to course materials, learning activities, assessments, and instructional resources through Moodle. The platform provides a centralized online learning environment where students can engage with content and track their progress throughout the certificate program.

Additional support may include technical assistance, instructor communication, course guidance, and access to LSU Online resources related to enrollment and online learning. Learners should review program materials for specific support services available during their courses.

About This Course

Prepare for smarter financial decisions in entrepreneurship

What You'll Learn

  • Understand and explain the assumptions and principles that underlie Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP)
  • Understand and explain the rules of double-entry accounting
  • Calculate the balance of the account
  • Define the steps that make up the accounting cycle
  • Describe the relationship between the major financial statements that make up an annual report
  • Explain the role of finance in the entrepreneurial process
  • Understand the importance of budgeting and financial planning
  • Identify and explain the three major pricing strategies
  • Define and distinguish between fixed and variable costs
  • Calculate the components of a master (static) budget
  • Prepare a cash budget
  • Prepare and analyze pro forma financial statements
  • Develop a basic financial plan
  • Use pro forma financial statements to evaluate a new venture
  • Explain the factors and assumptions involved in break-even analysis
  • Calculate Return on Investment (ROI) and payback period

Skills in this Course

  • GAAP principles & financial reporting
  • Double-entry accounting
  • Accounting cycle & account balances
  • Financial statement analysis
  • Entrepreneurial finance fundamentals
  • Budgeting & financial planning
  • Pricing strategy
  • Fixed & variable cost analysis
  • Master & cash budgeting
  • Pro forma financial statements
  • Break-even analysis
  • ROI & payback period

Frequently Asked Questions

What will I learn in this course?

This course provides a practical introduction to accounting and finance concepts that every entrepreneur should understand. You'll learn how to read financial statements, create budgets, analyze costs, develop financial forecasts, evaluate business opportunities, and use financial data to make informed business decisions.

Do I need prior accounting or finance experience?

No prior accounting or finance experience is required. The course is designed for entrepreneurs, aspiring business owners, and professionals who want to build a strong foundation in business finance, regardless of their background.

Who should take this course?

This course is ideal for entrepreneurs, startup founders, small business owners, and professionals who want to better understand the financial side of running a business. It's also valuable for anyone considering launching a new venture and wanting to make more confident financial decisions.

How is the course delivered?

The course is offered in a flexible, 100% online format that allows you to learn at your own pace. Course materials include readings, interactive activities, case studies, videos, knowledge checks, and other resources designed to reinforce key concepts and support real-world application.

Can this course help me evaluate whether a business idea is financially viable?

Yes. The course covers essential tools used to assess business opportunities, including budgeting, pricing strategies, cost analysis, break-even analysis, financial forecasting, return on investment (ROI), and payback period calculations. These skills can help you determine whether a business idea is financially sustainable before investing significant time or resources.

Will I have access to instructor support?

Yes. The course includes access to an "Ask the Expert" feature that allows you to submit questions to a subject matter expert throughout your learning experience. Responses are typically provided within 24 hours, helping you stay on track as you progress through the course.

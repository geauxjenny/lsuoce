Frequently Asked Questions

What is OLLI?

OLLI — the Osher Lifelong Learning Institute at LSU — is a membership-based community for adults age 50 and better who want to keep learning. Courses cover a wide range of topics, from fitness and arts to history and current events, in a relaxed, social environment.

Do I need to be an OLLI member to register?

OLLI membership is required to enroll in OLLI courses. If you are not yet a member, you can join when you register. Visit the OLLI at LSU website for current membership options and benefits.

Where does this class meet?

This section meets in person at West Feliciana Parish Library. Please arrive a few minutes early for your first class. Parking and room details will be shared after registration.

What is the refund policy?

OLLI course fees are generally non-refundable after the class begins. For cancellation requests before the start date, contact LSU Continuing Education. See our policies and registration page for full details.

Who can I contact with questions?

For registration help or course inquiries, contact LSU Continuing Education at answers@outreach.lsu.edu or call 833-280-5634.
